Blockchain Researcher

As a Blockchain Researcher, you will:
  • Conduct cutting-edge research to solve the pressing challenges for Zilliqa and blockchains in general.
  • Work closely with other team members to develop research outcomes into product-level implementation and verify assumptions and hypotheses with experiments.
  • Writing and publishing documents and papers to detail the research findings and experimental results.
  • Work closely with collaborators in the community or partner enterprises.
Qualifications
  • PhD degree in computer science, computer engineering, or equivalent practical experience.
  • Excellent track record of publications in international conferences and journals in one or more of the following domains: cybersecurity, blockchains, distributed systems and storage, applied cryptography, privacy preserving (or enhancing) technologies, computer networks, formal methods, programming languages.
  • Ability to work independently and collaboratively in an agile team.
